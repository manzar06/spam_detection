2Ô∏è‚É£ Project Description

Spam emails are unwanted messages that often contain advertisements, fake offers, or malicious links. Instead of using machine learning, this project uses a rule-based and keyword-matching approach to detect spam emails.

The system analyzes the content of an email and checks for:

Spam keywords

Suspicious links

Excessive capital letters

Repeated special characters


Based on predefined rules and scores, the email is classified as Spam or Not Spam (Ham).
